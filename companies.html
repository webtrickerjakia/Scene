What makes us different

Free hire replacements

If a new hire doesn’t pass their six-month probation, we’ll replace them for free. If we still don’t find you the right person, we’ll work for free until we have.

Bonuses tied to happy clients

Unlike other recruiters, we tie 100% of our team’s bonuses to your customer experience. Because if you’re not happy, we’re not happy.

Learn from our community
